The Space

We have one huge room with many couches and mattresses. Sometimes we have another room where more people can sleep when it's available.

If it's too much for you to sleep in one room with so many people but you'd still like to experience the community, you're welcome to come visit anyway anytime. If you want to stay with us in the same house but have a room for yourself, that's also possible if you can contribute in some way to the project (for example monetarily or by helping us with any of the tasks). Evening Check-in: To guarantee a place to sleep, you must be in the house by 8 PM every evening. If you can't make it before then, please arrange with someone in the community to ensure you have a spot. If no one can confirm this for you, then please find another place to stay for that night.

Morning Routine: We wake up early, between 6-8 am, and clean the house together for 1‚Äì2 hours. Please join in and help keep our shared space tidy and welcoming.
