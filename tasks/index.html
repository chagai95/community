Why These Tasks Matter

Liking, leaving reviews, and using emojis builds trust in our project when others see it. It pushes the Instagram or Google algorithm and feels good to show appreciation in a public form. It's fun and creates dopamine kicks for our brains to continue this project!

If these tasks don't appeal to you, we'd love to understand why so we can better accommodate your preferences. Some common reasons people prefer different tasks:

You avoid social media as a moral standpoint

You experience anxiety about friends seeing your online activity

You're uncomfortable promoting something you're not yet 100% connected to

You generally try to minimize technology use

You're focused on your own priorities with limited time

You're experiencing information overload

If you can't find anything in these tasks that you're comfortable with, that's okay. Please understand it's difficult to run a community like this. We need to prioritize people who help maintain our community in some way, so we don't feel our energy is being drained.
