Before Asking a Question...

Our community is here to help, but we need your help too! Many common questions have already been answered in our FAQ section.

Why check the FAQ first?

  • You'll likely get an immediate answer instead of waiting for a response
  • It helps preserve the group's energy for unique questions
  • It keeps discussions focused on new issues

When you visit the FAQ page:

  1. Use the search box to find relevant keywords
  2. Browse through existing questions
