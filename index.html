🏠 House Etiquette 🏠

😌 Feel at Home

As soon as you arrive, please feel and act like you already live here for a long time. From the beginning, you're part of the group and not like a guest!

🛌 Bedding

  • Take fresh sheets from the shelf when you arrive.
  • Use the same for the entirety of your stay.
  • When you're done with them, put them into the blue "dirty bag" - regardless of when you leave.
  • If you are leaving early in the morning make sure your best buddy takes care of this for you.
  • Do not use a mattress/pillow/blanket/duvet without a sheet/case/cover!

🤼 Shared Responsibilities

We expect everyone to contribute to household tasks, which include:

  • Using the dishwasher, tidying up the clean things inside and refilling it with used items.
  • Cleaning the kitchen and bathroom, using the vacuum cleaner in all areas.
  • Doing laundry (washing, drying, folding):
  • Bringing out the trash:

🛀 Bathroom Etiquette

  • Please take your towels with you to the room and do not leave them in the bathroom to dry!
  • It would be amazing if you clean any hair from the shower after every single time, even if it's not your hair.

💼 Luggage Storage

  • Please always bring your luggage to the basement.
  • Only keep your daily needed items in the shared room.

♥️ Community Spirit

We expect everyone to help each other and not hesitate to ask for help 💖

For example: "Where are the sheets?" "Where is a good place to go sightseeing?" "Which supermarket is cheapest?"
